My projector is named Health Monitoring Portal which has four modules Admin Patient Hospital and Doctor. Nowadays the medical records of the patient are written in a kind of paper. Which we know as file in our language. In this project we want to remove such file system and bring everything on online platform. Like patient's medical records, medicine, feature treatment etc. For example, if the patient is a resident of Surat, he has previously undergone any kind of surgery, then due to this surgery there is any problem or side effect in the future. But he currently lives in Ahmedabad and his medical record file is Surat, then to quickly resolve this side effect, the doctor wants information of the previous surgery or asks for the file. But he does not have it. So all these facilities are available in our project, online medical records, medicine etc.  So this doctor will start the further treatment after seeing it with ease. This will also make the patient's future bright and his treatment will be done easily without any difficulty. This is my main objective of creating the project.
